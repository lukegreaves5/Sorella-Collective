FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When do you do trials and where?

Trials are done in our studio located inside of Elephants Trunk, Mount Kisco, NY. We offer trials on Sundays or Mondays.

How long is a trial?

We leave up to 2 hours of trial time, this way we can play around with different ideas. We highly recommend booking your trial on the same day as a dress fitting if possible!

How do I book my date?

The date is not officially reserved until a deposit is made at the time of booking. Initial deposits are non refundable but will be deducted from the total cost on your day. Deposits are made via: Venmo, Zelle or PayPal.

How should I pay day of?

Balance due on day of services should be provided in cash.

What is the minimum # of girls to style a bridal party?

We require a 6 person minimum

Do you provide hair extensions?

Hair extensions must be purchased by the bride. If you aren’t familiar with clip in extensions or aren’t sure where to purchase them, ask us, we can help you!

How long does each service take?

On average 1 hour per bridesmaid and up to 1.5 hours for a bride.
